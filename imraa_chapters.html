IMRAA Chapters

IIMRAA has chapters (IMRAA chapters are clubs/student-led organizations) in colleges and high schools around the world in order to implement female empowerment initiatives and grant girls the chance to lead. IMRAA is a growing network, with chapters now at our partner schools in Ghana, at Thomas A. Edison High School in Virginia, at the University of Virginia, at the University of Dayton, and beyond. The IMRAA chapter at The College of William & Mary served as our pilot chapter for the 2018-2019 school year. Led by students, they hosted many "Sisters Discuss" sessions on prevalent, universal themes of females’ lives and gave a voice to a diverse range of females on campus. They held guest-speaker sessions and movie nights with relevant messages to IMRAA’s cause.
